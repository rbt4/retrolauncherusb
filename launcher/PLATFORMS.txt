PLUGCADE PLATFORM ADAPTERS
==========================

Enabled by default:
DOS, DOS CD-ROM, Arcade/MAME, NES, SNES, Nintendo 64, Game Boy, Game Boy Color,
Game Boy Advance, Genesis/Mega Drive, Master System, Game Gear, PC Engine,
Atari 2600, and PlayStation 1.

Optional adapters available from CHOOSE SYSTEMS:
Virtual Boy, Sega 32X, Sega CD, Atari 7800, Atari Lynx, Neo Geo Pocket,
WonderSwan, ColecoVision, Intellivision, MSX, and Commodore Amiga.

Plugcade creates matching folders under:

  DROP_GAMES_HERE\SYSTEM
  Library\SYSTEM
  Emulators\SYSTEM
  Saves\SYSTEM

Why adapters instead of one enormous emulator bundle?

- Old computers need frozen emulator builds appropriate to their Windows and CPU.
- Some emulators cannot legally or safely be redistributed as one package.
- Some systems require a user-supplied BIOS.
- A configurable adapter keeps the launcher and library portable and lightweight.

The launcher does not promise that every enabled system will run on every PC.
It provides the organization and launch wiring; the chosen emulator determines
actual console compatibility and performance.
